Emergency Situations

I need housing tonight - can you help?

Call us immediately: (310) 488-5280

Yes, we can often provide same-day housing for crisis situations. Our intake team will:

  • Assess your immediate needs over the phone
  • Check current bed availability
  • Arrange emergency admission if space allows
  • Connect you with other emergency resources if we're full

Don't sleep on the street - call now, even if it's 3am.

What if I'm having a mental health crisis?

We have 24/7 crisis counselors on staff. If you're in immediate danger:

Immediate Emergency:

Call 911 or go to nearest ER

Crisis Support:

Call us: (310) 488-5280

Our trained staff can provide immediate support and connect you with mental health services.

I'm being evicted tomorrow - what can I do?

Call us immediately - we can help!

Eviction situations are emergencies. We can:

  • Provide immediate temporary housing
  • Connect you with legal aid for eviction defense
  • Help with emergency rental assistance programs
  • Start your application for long-term transitional housing

Time is critical - call now: (310) 488-5280

No income required to get housing!

We serve people at all income levels:
