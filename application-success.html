Application Received!

Thank you for taking this important step toward stability and independence.

What Happens Next?

1

Application Review (24-48 hours)

Our admissions team will carefully review your application and assess how we can best support your needs.

2

Phone Screening

We'll call you to discuss your situation, answer questions, and schedule an in-person assessment if appropriate.

3

In-Person Assessment

Meet with our team, tour the facility, and ensure Forward Horizon is the right fit for your journey.

4

Move-In Coordination

If approved, we'll work with you to coordinate your move-in date and help you get settled.
