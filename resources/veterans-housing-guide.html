Emergency Housing Resources

National Call Center for Homeless Veterans

Phone: 1-877-4AID-VET (1-877-424-3838)

24/7 hotline providing immediate assistance and local resource connections

VA Homeless Program

Website: va.gov/homeless

Comprehensive housing assistance including:

  • Emergency shelter placement
  • Transitional housing programs
  • Permanent supportive housing
  • Rapid rehousing assistance

Financial Assistance Programs

HUD-VASH (Housing Choice Vouchers)

Rental assistance vouchers specifically for veterans experiencing homelessness

Contact: Your local VA Medical Center

SSVF (Supportive Services for Veteran Families)

Prevents homelessness and helps veterans obtain housing including:

  • Rental assistance
  • Security deposits
  • Utility payments
  • Moving costs

California-Specific Resources

CalVet Home Loans

Phone: 1-877-CALVET-1

Low-interest home loans for qualifying California veterans

Los Angeles Homeless Services Authority (LAHSA)

Phone: 213-683-3333

Coordinated Entry System for housing placement in LA County

How to Apply

Step 1: Contact the National Call Center for immediate assistance

Step 2: Visit your nearest VA Medical Center or Vet Center

Step 3: Complete coordinated entry assessment

Step 4: Apply for specific programs based on eligibility
