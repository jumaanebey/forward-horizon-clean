Application Timeline & What to Expect

Forward Horizon Transitional Housing for Veterans
Your journey to stable housing starts here

Important: We typically respond within 24 hours of receiving your application. If you don't hear from us, please call (310) 488-5280.

Application Process Timeline

Day 1

Initial Application Review

What happens: We review your application and conduct initial eligibility screening

You can expect:

  • Email confirmation of receipt within 2 hours
  • Phone call within 24 hours to discuss your situation
  • Initial questions about your housing needs and timeline
Days 2-3

Documentation & Assessment

What happens: We help you gather required documents and complete assessments

You'll need:

  • DD-214 or military discharge paperwork
  • Photo identification
  • Social Security card
  • Income documentation (if any)
  • Medical records (if applicable)
Days 4-7

In-Person Interview

What happens: Face-to-face meeting to discuss your goals and our program

We'll cover:

  • Your housing history and current situation
  • Personal goals for independent living
  • Any support services you might need
  • Program rules and expectations
  • Tour of available housing units
Days 8-14

Final Review & Decision

What happens: Our team reviews your application and makes a placement decision

Possible outcomes:

  • Approved: Immediate housing placement available
  • Waitlist: Approved but waiting for unit availability
  • Referred: Connected to more appropriate services
Move-in Day

Welcome to Forward Horizon

What happens: Complete move-in process and begin your journey

You'll receive:

  • Keys to your furnished unit
  • Program handbook and guidelines
  • Introduction to support staff
  • Resource list for local services
  • Your first case management appointment

Program Requirements

To be eligible, you must:

  • Be a U.S. military veteran with honorable discharge
  • Be experiencing homelessness or at risk of homelessness
  • Be committed to working toward independent living
  • Agree to participate in case management services
  • Be substance-free or actively participating in treatment
  • Have no history of violent crimes or sex offenses

What We Provide

Your furnished unit includes:

  • Private bedroom in shared housing
  • Fully equipped kitchen access
  • Bathroom facilities
  • Utilities included (electric, water, internet)
  • Laundry facilities
  • 24/7 on-site support

Support services include:

  • Weekly case management meetings
  • Job placement assistance
  • Benefits enrollment help
  • Life skills workshops
  • Mental health referrals
  • Transportation assistance

Preparing for Success

While waiting for placement:

  • Gather all required documentation
  • Consider your long-term housing goals
  • Research job opportunities in your field
  • Connect with VA benefits if you haven't already
  • Maintain regular contact with our team
